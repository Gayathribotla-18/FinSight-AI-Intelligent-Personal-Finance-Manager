# FinSight AI – Intelligent Personal Finance Manager

## Project Vision

FinSight AI is an AI-powered personal finance management platform that helps users track income and expenses, manage budgets, analyze spending habits, and receive personalized AI-driven financial recommendations.

---

## Objectives

- Manage income and expenses
- Track monthly budgets
- Visualize financial analytics
- Generate AI financial insights
- Export financial reports

---
